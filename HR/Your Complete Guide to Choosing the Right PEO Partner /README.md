**PEO vs. EOR: Choosing the Appropriate Employment Model**
Understanding the distinction between a [PEO and an Employer of Record (EOR)](https://www.payroll2bangladesh.com/peo-services/)  is essential, as each addresses different workforce requirements. A PEO operates through co-employment arrangements—you and the provider jointly fulfill employer obligations. This typically suits domestic workforce management best.
An EOR, conversely, assumes complete legal employer responsibility. While this arrangement means somewhat reduced direct HR oversight, it eliminates legal liability from your organization entirely, making it the optimal solution for managing employees across international borders.
